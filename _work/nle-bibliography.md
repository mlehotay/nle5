# NLE Bibliography

Working bibliography for papers cited by the old Facebook NLE README and the
current NetHack-LE NLE README.

Sources:

- Old NLE README: <https://github.com/facebookresearch/nle/blob/main/README.md>
- Current NLE README: <https://github.com/NetHack-LE/nle/blob/main/README.md>

## Core NLE Papers

- Heinrich Kuttler, Nantas Nardelli, Alexander H. Miller, Roberta Raileanu,
  Marco Selvatici, Edward Grefenstette, and Tim Rocktaschel.
  [The NetHack Learning Environment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2006.13760).
  NeurIPS 2020.

- Eric Hambro, Roberta Raileanu, Danielle Rothermel, Vegard Mella,
  Tim Rocktaschel, Heinrich Kuttler, and Naila Murray.
  [Dungeons and Data: A Large-Scale NetHack Dataset](https://papers.neurips.cc/paper_files/paper/2022/file/9d9258fd703057246cb341e615426e2d-Paper-Datasets_and_Benchmarks.pdf).
  NeurIPS Datasets and Benchmarks 2022.

## Papers Using NLE

- Giulio Paglieri et al.
  [BALROG: Benchmarking Agentic LLM and VLM Reasoning On Games](https://arxiv.org/abs/2411.13543).
  ICLR 2025.

- Martin Klissarov et al.
  [MaestroMotif: Skill Design from Artificial Intelligence Feedback](https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.08542).
  ICLR 2025.

- Martin Klissarov et al.
  [Motif: Intrinsic Motivation from Artificial Intelligence Feedback](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.00166).
  ICLR 2024.

- Izumiya and Simo-Serra.
  [Inventory Management with Attention-Based Meta Actions](https://esslab.jp/~ess/publications/IzumiyaCOG2021.pdf).
  CoG 2021.

- Mikayel Samvelyan et al.
  [MiniHack the Planet: A Sandbox for Open-Ended Reinforcement Learning Research](https://arxiv.org/abs/2109.13202).
  NeurIPS 2021.

- Tianjun Zhang et al.
  [BeBold: Exploration Beyond the Boundary of Explored Regions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.08621).
  December 2020.
